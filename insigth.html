Marine AIS

3 October, 2018

What is Marine Automatic Identification System. Marine Automatic Identification System (AIS) is a system that transmits tracking information of ships and used by vessel tracking services(VTS). When satellites are used to detect signals the term Satellite-AIS (S-AIS) is used to differentiate from signals received by terrestrial coastal watch stations. A ship’s AIS signature includes the unique identification code, position, course and speed as well as destination and estimated time of arrival displayed on a screen.
